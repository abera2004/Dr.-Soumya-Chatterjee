About

Dr. Soumya Chatterjee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Institute of Technology, Durgapur. He received his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata (2019), MSc in Electrical Power Engineering from Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany (2014), and B.E. in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University (2009). His professional journey spans academia and industry with expertise in high-voltage engineering, condition monitoring, and applied signal processing.
